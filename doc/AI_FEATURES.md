# SupplySync AI — AI Features

## Core AI (Differentiators)
- AI-powered supplier recommendations based on performance and market conditions.
- Predictive risk analytics for supplier disruptions and financial issues.
- Dynamic pricing intelligence with market benchmarking.
- NLP contract analysis for clause extraction and risk identification.
- Automated negotiation assistance with pricing insights.
- Advanced analytics for spend optimization and consolidation.

## Extended AI (Future)
- AI-powered supplier discovery and vetting.
- Predictive demand planning with auto-adjusted ordering.
- Social listening for supplier sentiment.
- Automated carbon footprint calculation and optimization.
- Financial health monitoring using real-time data sources.
- Digital twin simulation for scenario planning.
