# SupplySync AI — User Flows

## Supplier Onboarding
1. Internal user creates supplier invite.
2. Supplier completes profile and uploads documents.
3. Compliance review and approval.
4. Supplier activated and available for procurement.

## Purchase Order Workflow
1. Requester creates PO draft.
2. Workflow routes approvals based on rules.
3. PO issued to supplier.
4. Goods received and logged.

## Invoice Matching
1. Supplier submits invoice.
2. System matches PO, receipt, invoice.
3. Exceptions routed for review.
4. Approved invoice sent to payments.

## Contract Lifecycle
1. Upload or draft contract.
2. Review and approvals.
3. Active monitoring and renewal alerts.
4. Expiry or renewal.

## RFQ / Bid Management
1. Create RFQ with items and requirements.
2. Invite suppliers and collect bids.
3. Compare bids and award.

## Supplier Performance
1. Track KPIs and scorecards.
2. Generate performance reviews.
3. Create corrective action plans.

## Risk & Compliance
1. Monitor certifications and policy compliance.
2. Run periodic risk assessments.
3. Trigger alerts and remediation actions.

## Budget Control
1. Create department budgets.
2. Monitor spend vs budget.
3. Enforce approval thresholds.
