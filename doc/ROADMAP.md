# SupplySync AI — Roadmap

## Phase 0 — Current Baseline
- Auth, tenant bootstrap, supplier CRUD, documents, basic dashboard, AI insights page.

## Phase 1 — MVP Scope
- Supplier database and profiles
- Basic PO workflow
- Simple approval process
- Supplier performance tracking
- Document storage and search
- Basic reporting

## Phase 2 — Core Must-Have Completion
- Supplier onboarding workflows
- Contract lifecycle management
- Invoice processing and three-way matching
- Supplier risk assessment
- Spend analytics dashboards
- Supplier communication portal
- Catalog management
- RFQ/RFP management
- Compliance tracking
- Payment processing integration
- Full RBAC and audit trails

## Phase 3 — Important Enhancements
- Mobile approvals and communication
- Inventory integration
- Budget management and controls
- Multi-currency support
- Email notifications

## Phase 4 — Advanced Differentiators
- AI supplier recommendations
- Predictive risk analytics
- Dynamic pricing intelligence
- NLP contract analysis
- Supply chain mapping & visualization
- Automated negotiation assistance
- Collaborative planning tools
- Advanced analytics & BI
- Crisis management & continuity planning
- ESG monitoring and smart contract automation
- IoT integration
- Blockchain verification

## Phase 5 — Innovation Track
- AI supplier discovery
- Predictive demand planning
- AR inventory interface
- Social listening
- Carbon footprint optimization
- Voice procurement
- Gamification
- Satellite/geopolitical intelligence
- Financial health monitoring
- Digital twin simulations
